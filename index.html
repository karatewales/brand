Brand Guidelines

The purpose of this page is to provide guidance on how logos, colors and fonts should be used to provide a consistent look and feel across all media.

Colours

Green : #018d39

Red : #ec032e

Fonts

For general copy etc in order of preference:

  • Helvetica
  • Arial

Helvetica is the default font because it is designed to be easily readable on screen and in printed media. Helvetica is also available by default in the majority of browsers and devices. Arial can be used in its place if its not available.

Serif fonts e.g. Times New Roman should be not used at any time.

General Rules

  • Spacing should be provided from other elements. On screen this should be at least 25px and on print at least 1cm.
  • The logo should never be merged, rotated, twisted, re-coloured or combined with another logo.
